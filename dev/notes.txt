SPANISH VERB TENSES
=================================================================================
--1-BASE---------------------------------------------------------------------------
1INFI - 01 - infinitive              - to speak                        - hablar
1PRPA - 02 - present participle      - speaking                        - hablando
1PAPA - 03 - past participle         - spoke                           - hablado
--2-INDICATIVE---------------------------------------------------------------------
2PRES - 04 - present                 - I speak                         - hablo 
2IMPE - 05 - imperfect               - I was speaking, I used to speak - hablaba
2PRET - 06 - preterite               - I spoke                         - hablé
2PRPE - 07 - present perfect         - I have spoken                   - he hablado
2PAPE - 08 - past perfect            - I had spoken                    - había hablado
2PREP - 09 - preterite perfect       - I had spoken                    - hube hablado
2FUIN - 10 - future intention        - I am going to speak             - voy a hablar
2FUTU - 11 - future                  - I will speak                    - hablaré
2FUTP - 12 - future perfect          - I will have spoken              - habré hablado
2COND - 13 - conditional             - I would speak                   - hablaría
2CONP - 14 - conditional perfect     - I would have spoken             - habría hablado
--3-SUBJUNCTIVE--------------------------------------------------------------------
3PRES - 15 - present                 - I suppose I speak               - hable
3IMP1 - 16 - imperfect version 1     - I suppose I was speaking        - hablara
3IMP2 - 17 - imperfect version 2     - I suppose I was speaking        - hablase
3PRPE - 18 - present perfect         - I suppose I have spoken         - haya hablado
3PAP1 - 19 - past perfect version 1  - I suppose I had spoken          - hubiera hablado
3PAP2 - 20 - past perfect version 2  - I suppose I had spoken          - hubiese hablado
3FUTU - 21 - future                  - I suppose I will speak          - hablare
3FUTP - 22 - future perfect          - I suppose I will have spoken    - hubiere hablado
--4-IMPERATIVE---------------------------------------------------------------------
4AFFI - 23 - affirmative             - speak!                          - habla
4NEGA - 24 - negative                - don't speak!                    - no hables
===================================================================================
